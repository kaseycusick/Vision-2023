New Employee Onboarding Checklist

Step 1. Welcome

Congratulations and welcome to the Office of Enterpirse Technology! We're excited to have you on board. Your experience will help us fulfill the department's vision of using technology to power more efficient, compassionate, and fair government services in California.


In the upcoming days, and weeks, you will be welcomed by your onboarding buddy and team. We will also invite you to meet our Chief, and Deputy Chief Technology Innovation Officer; Rick Klau and Phoebe Peronto in thier open door sessions.


To learn more about us, feel free to review the CDT Organizational Guide

Step 2. Your first day

Today you will go in to the office to get set up with your equipment, and meet your team. To start, you will meet your onboarding buddy at the Prospect Green 1 (PG1) Building.

10860 Gold Center Dr. Rancho Cordova 95670

(images added soon...)

You will tour the building, get your equipment, badge and attend a virtual meeting with your team today (or in the first week).

Step 3. iCentral and the HR Portal

iCentral is the intranet for CDT, and the home for internal resources, including the HR Portal. The HR portal will be where you go to report your time and acknowledge your timesheet.

    • Step 6. In-depth training and support

    In the first 3 months you will continue to onboard by diving in to the team and your role. Your onboarding buddy will work with you and your peers to set up time to learn what your team does and where you will fit in. This will include smaller team meetings and 1-on-1's with your peers.
