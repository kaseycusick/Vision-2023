Hiring Manager Play

Use this play to begin the onboarding process for new hires, and orient your new hire to the team and their role.

New Employee Onboarding Checklist

Running the play

This play requires collaboration with the onboarding buddy, make sure you share documents.


The Onboarding Process for hiring managers comprises three phases:

  • After the offer is accepted
  • One week before the start date
  • New hire milestones

    • Phase 1. After the offer is accepted

    This is exciting! Your new employee has accepted the job offer and is planning to join the team. Make their experience as smooth as possible by preparint for their arrival.

    What you need to do.

    • Submit staff movement service request
    • Start the Employee Checklist
    • Add new employee to email distribution lists
    • Assign onboarding buddy
    • Submit equipment request

    Phase 2. One week before the start date

    Wow, time flies! Your new employee starts next week and these tasks should be completed one week before their start date.

    What you need to do.

    • Send employee welcome email
    • Submit employee's signed telework form
    • Schedule team meet-and-greet
    • Schedule 30 min 1-on-1 to discuss upcoming projects/meetings to add to their calendar
    • Coordinate with OET Admin to add open door meetings to new employee calendar
    • Schedule group meeta nd greet with Rick and Phoebe

    Phase 3. New hire milestones

    First Day

    • Send welcome announcment in Teams
    • Send the employee training email

    First Week

    • Manager welcome
    • High level overview
    • Onboarding checklist
    • Encourage to attend open door meeting

    90-day mark

    • Employee will receive general training, and then more in-depth training
    • Review role and expectations
    • Check in monthly
    • Recommend training in PluralSite, PACE, etc.
